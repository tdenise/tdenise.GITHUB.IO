How does shAIRsky optimize air travel?

We optimize air travel by providing a platform that alleviates common issue that flyers encounter with
air travel such as navigating the airport, switching flights, and viewing flight information quickly.

Have trouble navigating
unfamiliar airports?

shAIRsky is a mobile platform that helps users navigate airports through Augmented Reality navigation.

  • Using AR, all you need to do is point your phone in front of you.
  • shAIRsky will generate arrows and directions to get you where you need to be.
  • We also offer a 2D map of the entire scope of the airport with a tap of a button!

Need to check flight information quickly?

Input your flight information and have real-time updated flight info.

  • Get notifications when flight info is updated so you never miss a flight.
  • shAIRsky predicts your travel time and accounts for security, check-in, and travel time using Artificial Intelligence

Is switching flights a pain?

shAIRsky aims to maximize convenience by suggesting travel alternates.

  • Changing flights can be inconvenient so we thought of a way for you to get the info easily!
  • shAIRsky shows you flight options so you can switch with ease.

Technology

shAIRsky utilizes multiple technologies to bring ease of mind to their users during their travels.

  • Beacon Technology

    - Easier and more accurate location based searching

    - Wireless transmitters that use low-energy Bluetooth technology to send signals to other smart devices nearby

    - Potential for proximity based marketing

  • Augmented Reality

    - AR generated images created using Unity

    - Utilizes phone functionalities such as camera, geolocation, accelerometer, and gyroscope.

  • Artificial Intelligence

    - Predict travel time accounting for security, check-in, and travel time using airport and local airport business datasets.

    - Utilizing Tensorflow, neural networks can be built to predict travel times.
