shairSky Rundown

Problem Statement

How might we optimize air travel by providing a platform that alleviates common flyer travel issues such as navigating unfamiliar airports, switching flights, and accessing flight information?

What is the problem/opportunity?

Navigating an airport is difficult.

What is it's impact?

- Eases stress associated with the air travel experience

- With the utilization of beacon technology airport businesses can benefit from proximity-based offers as well as discounts.

What is the solution?

A mobile application that helps you navigate the airport, suggests flights you can swap into, and flight info checking functionality.

How does the solution address the problem/opportunity?

Social and community. Eventually be turned into a share economy, sustainability. Reduces stress associated with air travel.

How do we make it happen?

AR, AI, Beacon Tech
